## Rules

1. **No hype words.** No "revolutionary", no "game-changer", no "AI-powered" without saying what model.
2. **Show, then tell.** Every post leads with a screenshot or a demo URL anyone can run themselves.
3. **Don't punch down at incumbents.** Quad9 and NextDNS are good products; we just do something they don't. Respect, not snark.
4. **Be ready to support.** Day-of-launch on-call needs ≥3 people online for at least 12 hours.
