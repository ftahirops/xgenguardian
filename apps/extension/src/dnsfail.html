This is NOT an XGenGuardian block. The URL is not on any threat list — your computer's network just couldn't resolve it. This usually means one of the things below.

Common causes

  • The domain doesn't exist (typo in the URL, or the site has been taken down)
  • Your DNS provider (Pi-hole, NextDNS, AdGuard Home, ISP filter) is blocking it
  • Your Internet connection is offline
  • The site's authoritative DNS servers are down

Common causes

  • The server is offline or rebooting
  • A firewall (yours or the site's) is blocking the connection
  • The site is overloaded and not accepting new connections
  • Your network is offline
